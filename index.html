Our Heritage

The RSA Story

◆
46Years

Founded in 1978 by Shri Rameshwar Lal in the heart of Indore's legendary Sarafa Bazaar, RSA Jewellers has been synonymous with purity, trust, and timeless beauty for over four decades.

Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the weight of tradition and the shine of modernity — BIS hallmarked, ethically sourced, and meticulously crafted by artisans who have inherited their skills through generations.

From a bridal necklace to a simple silver anklet, we bring the same love and attention to every creation — because jewellery is not just an ornament, it is a memory.
